# The Pirate Game
In The Pirate Game, you play as a pirate searching for a legendary chest on a mysterious, changing island.
You start by finding your landing spot, then move across the map to dig for gold and hidden items.
The game is a race against time and nature: the treasure actually moves if you take too long, 
and the ocean water slowly rises to drown the land.
To survive and win, you must manage your energy by eating coconuts, 
use a metal detector to track down the chest, and decide when to use powerful dynamite.
Itâ€™s a fun mix of exploration and strategy where you try to get the highest score before the island disappears!

# Features

1. Inability to dig the same place twice and leaving dig holes on those places
2. Dynamite that can be used by pressing B and digs a region of 3x3
3. The pirate can escape if he is surrounded by obstacles through dug holes, but can't get back inside
4. Rising water level by 1 tile every 10 pirate actions in which pirate can be drowned
5. Moving treasure to a random position every 8 pirate actions
6. Colourful text for more comfortable experience
7. Inventory with ability to leave coconuts near hut for additional score
8. Scanner that can be used through Inventory to find the distance to the closest gold coin or treasure
9. Writing what items the pirate missed at the end
10. Auto generated map that is shown in real time with either round shape, ar randomised
11. Different levels of difficulty that affect the map generation
12. Cool animations of map generation
13. Map parts can be revealed with a chance of 20% when digging
